Change Healthcare

In February 2024, Change Healthcare, a subsidiary of UnitedHealth Group and a major processor of U.S. medical claims, fell victim to a ransomware attack. The attackers, the BlackCat (ALPHV) group, infiltrated the company's systems. They exfiltrated sensitive data and deployed ransomware that crippled operations.

SnowFlake

Snowflake, the prominent cloud data platform, experienced a significant data breach in May 2024. The data exfiltration activity via Snowflake affected over 100 of its customers, including major corporations like AT&T, Ticketmaster, and Santander Bank.

UK Ministry of Defence

In May 2024, the UK's Ministry of Defence (MoD) experienced a significant data breach when a contractor-operated payroll system was compromised by a cyber attack. This system contained personal information—including names, bank details, and, in some cases, home addresses—of approximately 270,000 current and former UK military personnel.
